GEMSBOKS

The gemsbok is a species of antepole that lives in the kalahari and namib dessert of Southern africa.they are characterized by several visual features: long, slim and spiral horns.

it is one of the few antepole species where female trophies are sometimes more desirable than male ones.

Where will you find them?

You will find gemsbok near panda's canyon
