Coffee Shop

This newly opened roastery and coffee shop on Davenport Road in Durban offers a variety of imported and local green bean coffees. The people at The Bean Green take their coffee very seriously, with coffee sourced from the highest altitude African plantations. Freshly ground beans and filtered water are used and coffee is made by a fully trained barista. The beans are roasted to your preference and available for sale in vacuum packed packs.
